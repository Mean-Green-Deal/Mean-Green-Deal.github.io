PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate)

Commonly found in: - Soft drink bottles - Water bottles - Ketchup bottles - Beer bottles - Mouthwash bottles - Peanut butter cantainers - Salad dressing bottles - Vegetable oil bottles To Recycle: Empty and rinse bottle and remove cap unless it states otherwise before recycling.

♲

HDPE (High Density Polyethylene)

Commonly found in: - Milk jugs - Juice bottles - Bleach containers - Detergent containers - Shampoo bottles - Trash bags - Shopping bags - Motor oil bottles - Butter containers - Yogurt containers - Cereal box liners To Recycle: - Some places may deny containers without necks. Plastic bags cannot be recycled but some stores will accept them.
