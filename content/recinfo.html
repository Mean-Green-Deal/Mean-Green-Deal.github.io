Recycling Information

♲

PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate)

Commonly found in:
- Soft drink bottles
- Water bottles
- Ketchup bottles
- Beer bottles
- Mouthwash bottles
- Peanut butter cantainers
- Salad dressing bottles
- Vegetable oil bottles
To Recycle:
Empty and rinse bottle and remove cap unless it states otherwise before recycling.

♲

HDPE (High Density Polyethylene)

Commonly found in:
- Milk jugs
- Juice bottles
- Bleach containers
- Detergent containers
- Shampoo bottles
- Trash bags
- Shopping bags
- Motor oil bottles
- Butter containers
- Yogurt containers
- Cereal box liners
To Recycle:
- Some places may deny containers without necks. Plastic bags cannot be recycled but some stores will accept them.

♲

PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)

Commonly found in:
- Blister packaging
- Wire jacketing
- Siding
- Windows
- Piping
To Recycle:
- Accepted by some plastic lumber makers otherwise rarely recycled.

♲

LDPE (Low Density Polyethylene)

Commonly found in:
- Squeeszable bottles
- Bread
- Frozen food
- Dry-cleaning
- Shopping bags
- Tote bags
- Furniture
To Recycle:
- Few communities accept this, otherwise throw in trash.
